Measure user satisfaction PURE Scores for each fundamental task that can be accomplished with that product Survey As needed The PURE score for a product is the sum of the scores for each fundamental task that can be accomplished with that product.
  • UserTesting
  • Qualtrics
  • Other
 The PURE method quantifies how difficult a product is to use and provides qualitative insights into how to fix it, both without costing a lot of time or money. A metric you can track over time to evaluate all the diffferent parts of your application, as well as an overall score.
